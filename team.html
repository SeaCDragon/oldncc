Team
Nathan Cheng

Principal

Nathan’s experience in construction and business management spans three decades. He began working in the building industry in 1989, and started developing commercial projects in 1995, including his own retail store and restaurants. In 2002, he launched a management consulting business, working with private companies, public agencies and community organizations. In this capacity, he worked in a wide array of settings, and managed commercial construction projects of varying budget and scope. Since 2013, he has focused on owner’s representation, expanding on his previous residential construction experience to include high-end residential projects with complex considerations.

Kurt Meiswinkel

Project Manager

Kurt has worked in the construction industry for over 40 years. Hailing from a family of builders, he entered the vocation as an apprentice, and over the course of his career, has mastered a variety of disciplines, including framing, insulation, drywall, taping, fireproofing, and exterior lath & plaster. He is the founder of several companies, including RFJ Meiswinkel and KMI (formerly KM Drywall), and has served principal and partnership roles in many others. He continues to promote innovations in the industry by such means as securing patents and developing techniques. Kurt’s construction expertise is both deep and extensive, making him an invaluable asset to any project team.

Alex Young

Project Manager

Alex began working in business analytics in 2013, in private and public agencies. From nonprofit organizations and public institutions to private software companies, Alex was responsible for ensuring that each business structure was meeting top output efficiencies. In 2016, he focused on project management leveraging his experiences and techniques of Business Process Management (BPM) to take on large-scale development and construction projects. He has a demonstrated ability to collaborate with multiple players across varied industries.

Jesse Mallory

Project Manager

Jesse developed his project management style during his decade-long span in various supervisory roles in an elite firefighting "hotshot" crew. After leaving his work in the forestry service, Jesse moved to San Francisco, obtained his general contractors license and continued his work in construction on many different high-end projects.

Kevin McCaffrey

Project Manager, License #871947

Kevin has 27 years of experience in construction trades and management in both residential and commercial arenas. He and his brother started a design-build firm in 2001, and as general contractors and project managers, guided their original designs from concept to completion. Kevin’s long-standing relationships with contractors and vendors, along with his focus on team communication and process have been key to his success in project management.

Daryl Soe

Project Manager

Daryl began his career in property management and office services, where he spent more than 10 years advancing his family business and managing the operations of a multi-tenant building. During the next 17 years, Daryl worked in the financial services industry, the last 11 of which, have been as a product and relationship manager, where he facilitated the growth of an industry-leading account platform 400% to $100 billion in assets under management.

John Somers

Project Manager

With a background in San Francisco Bay Area construction that spans over a quarter century, John has been involved in a wide array of commercial and residential projects. Drawing from his extensive experience as an independent contractor, John has managed project personnel and operational details.

Darren McCaffrey

Project Manager, License #871947

Darren has over 20 years of experience in residential design and construction, both interior and exterior, with a particular affinity for creating harmony between the two. He and his brother, Kevin, started a design-build firm in 2001 and, as general contractors and project managers through the design concept and completion.

Billy Schwartz

Project Manager

Billy has been in the construction and fine woodworking industries for over 18 years. Originally from New England, he learned traditional techniques and developed a love for beautifully crafted details. Since moving to the Bay Area, he has expanded his skills to include cabinetry and project management, and has developed a reputation among his peers as being highly proficient in his field.
